Module A

Research Design & Theory

This module defines the **conceptual foundation** of your research. If this step is weak, no amount of SmartPLS analysis can save your paper.

1. What Is a Research Model?

A research model is a **theory-driven representation** of how abstract concepts (latent variables) are expected to relate to one another.

In PLS-SEM, the model has two inseparable parts:

  • Measurement Model – how concepts are measured
  • Structural Model – how concepts influence each other
Key Idea
SmartPLS does not create theory. It only tests the theory you design here.
Theory First

2. Reflective vs. Formative Constructs

This is the most important conceptual decision in PLS-SEM. A wrong choice here leads to invalid results.

Simple Meaning

  • Reflective: The construct causes the indicators
  • Formative: The indicators create the construct

Reflective (Most Common)

Example: Perceived Usefulness
If usefulness increases, all related survey items increase.

Removing one item does NOT change the meaning.

Formative (Index)

Example: Service Quality
Punctuality, comfort, and safety together form the construct.

Removing one item CHANGES the meaning.

3. Real-World Research Example (Journal-Style)

Context: Low-Carbon Urban Mobility

Cities promote electric buses and integrated public transport, yet commuter adoption remains inconsistent.

Key Constructs

  • Perceived Usefulness (Reflective)
  • Environmental Concern (Reflective)
  • Service Quality (Formative)
  • Adoption Intention (Reflective)

Hypotheses

H1: Perceived Usefulness → Adoption Intention (+)
H2: Environmental Concern → Adoption Intention (+)
H3: Service Quality → Perceived Usefulness (+)
H4: Service Quality → Adoption Intention (+)

This model structure is commonly accepted in Q1 transportation, sustainability, and information systems journals.

4. How to Write Hypotheses Correctly

A good hypothesis must be:

  • Directional
  • Theory-based
  • Testable
Correct:
H1: Service Quality has a positive effect on Adoption Intention.
Wrong:
Service Quality is related to Adoption Intention.
