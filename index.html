Course Overview

SmartPLS 4.0 Complete Course

This interactive course provides a complete, step-by-step introduction to Partial Least Squares Structural Equation Modeling (PLS-SEM) using SmartPLS 4.0. The course is designed for beginners from any discipline and guides learners from theory formulation to full research paper preparation.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand theoretical foundations of PLS-SEM
  • Design conceptual and measurement models correctly
  • Apply SmartPLS 4.0 for empirical analysis
  • Interpret results using accepted thresholds
  • Prepare publishable research outputs
